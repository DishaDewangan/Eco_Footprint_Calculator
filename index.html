Welcome to the Eco Footprint Calculator

This tool helps you estimate your carbon footprint based on your daily activities. By understanding your impact on the environment, you can take steps to reduce it.

How to Use:

  • Car Travel: Enter the average distance you travel by car per week (in miles or kilometers).
  • Flights Per Year: Input the number of flights you take in a year. Consider both domestic and international flights.
  • Energy Consumption: Provide your monthly energy usage in kilowatt-hours (kWh). You can find this on your electricity bill.
  • Waste Generated: Estimate the amount of waste you generate per month in kilograms. Include general household waste.
